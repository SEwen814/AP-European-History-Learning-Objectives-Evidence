🎯 Unit 1 Learning Objectives

The Renaissance
✓ Explain how the revival of classical texts contributed to the development of the Renaissance (humanism, secularism, art)
✓ Analyze the impact of humanism on arts, literature, and education in Europe
✓ Assess how the Renaissance impacted social and gender roles
✓ Evaluate the methods used by monarchs to consolidate power during this period
Age of Exploration
✓ Identify and explain causes of European exploration and expansion
✓ Describe technological and navigational advances that facilitated overseas exploration
✓ Analyze the impact of the Columbian Exchange on Europe, Africa, and the Americas
✓ Compare the methods and motives of different European powers in their imperial ventures
Economic & Social Development
✓ Evaluate the effects of Commercial and Agricultural Revolutions on European economy and society
✓ Explain how changing economic practices contributed to the rise of capitalism and decline of feudalism
✓ Analyze demographic changes and their impact on European society and urbanization
